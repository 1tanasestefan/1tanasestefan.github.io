About Me

Tănase Ștefan-Daniel

Computer Science and Mathematics Student

I am a college student specializing in Mathematics and Computer Science, with a deep interest in web development, data analysis, and machine learning.

My journey in the tech world began with curiosity for problem-solving and maths. Through my studies, I aim to combine my mathematical foundation with programming expertise to tackle real-world challenges.

I began studying programming at 8 years old at a programming centre in Buzău, my home county, where I started by learning Scratch, a very kid-friendly visual programming language, which ignited my passion for programming. Then I began learning C and C++, which was quite a heavy step for a kid at the age of 11, but I adapted easily and loved to learn algorithms and to participate competitive programming contests such as OJI. Since then, I still love programming in C and C++, but also learned other languages such as Python, Kotlin and web development languages.

The passion for programming helped me develop problem-solving skills which were useful in mathematics for example, or made me discover new passions and hobbies such as solving various puzzles like the Rubik’s Cube.

I love learning and discovering new things and I am still in a continuous learning process.
